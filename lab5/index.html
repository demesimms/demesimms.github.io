Bio

I served as a GEOINT (Geospatial Intelligence) analyst over the course of 6 years with duty stations in South Korea and Italy. After the military I continued my career through military contracting and served a year in Afghanistan as a GMTI (Ground Moving Target Indicator). Currently I am pursuing a career in software engineering through PDX Code Guild in Portland, OR.

In my freetime I enjoy traveling and experiencing different cultures. I most recently lived in Zagreb, Croatia for a year. Previously, I have lived in South Korea for 2 years, Italy for 3 years, and have travelled to nearly 40 countries. When I am at home I enjoy spending time with my dogs and playing Call of Duty to stay up to date on my military skills. My biggests interests at the moment are learning and studying multiple programming languages to further develop my skills as a developer.
